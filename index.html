Welcome to Hat Scarf or a Badge

We are an independent outlet based in Anfield stocking a range of our own unique designs and limited edition T-shirts, Hats, Scarfs and Badges.

All our t-shirts are unofficial merchandise. We aim to offer a different option to the official LFC Merchandise and are proud to have had some past and present LFC players and managers. As well as some of the citys best boxers, TV personalties and musicians wearing our t-shirts.
